I am Ferit Yiğit BALABAN, Computer Engineering undergradute (currently in second grade) and a software engineer candidate.

growing up

I was in my four's when I was introduced to computers, and after two years of pondering around Windows Vista, I met the Internet. Oh, boy. From Flash games to the vast knowledge in Wikipedia, I was an explorer who've just dug out a small piece of an ancient city. Somewhere between my 10's to 12's, all this interest of anything and everything in the internet gradually shifted to the machine that brought what I ordered from it.

hello world.

Just dreaming and fantasizing about computers, and electricity of course, wasn't enough for me. That was the point which I came across programming. I was quickly interested in electronics and Arduinos! As a beginner, I was fluent in Scratch (lol) until I discovered C++, gave it a shot and got frustrated.

microsoft, <3 => </3

From 2019 to 2022, I developed many Windows desktop applications using C# with WinForms. Late in 2021, I started using Ubuntu (an GNU+Linux distro) which led me to experiment with the idea of cross-platform applications. Currently, I'm on an Arch Linux machine, with i3-gaps, polybar and kitty!

I do, and will do my part to support the “Free as in Freedom„ philosopy, and the community of Free and Open Source Software.

in conclusion.

I'm interested in web design and frontend development; machine learning, especially natural language processing; systems development and operating systems; mathematics. In addition to all those stuff that we can't touch with our fingers, I have a strong affection to electronics, processor design and logic circuits.
